    Alonso Like "Fine Wine"


    By Shoaib Jussab: P13194950



    Xabi Alonso is arguably in the form of his life at the Bavarians at the ripe old age of 35. The player is in the twilight years of his career and like "Fine Wine" seems to only get better.

    When Toni Kroos left Bayern in the summer, eyebrows were raised at his replacement. Xabi Alonso was bought in on a two year contract and has been brilliant since joining the current Bundesliga champions. Now in to his second season the World Cup winner talks about the secrets behind his immense form.

    Xabi Alonso states "There is no secret to my form, I just want to play well, do my best for my team mates and win trophies". Asked whether he has considered hanging up his boots he states "I am not thinking about that just yet, I want to play at the highest level for as long as possible".

    Alonso has been superb for many years, he is without doubt regarded as one of the best midfielders of his generation and so he should be. He has won trophies wherever he has been. His incredible passing range is second to none and at this rate long may his dazzling form continue.
